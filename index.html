WELCOME TO OUR PRACTICE DR. J. WHITLEY WILLS

Welcome to the office of Dr. J. Whitley Wills! Our dentist has been providing exeptional general, cosmetic, implant, restorative and preventive and preventative dentistry in Memphis, Tenessee, and the sorrounding areas since 1993, and he knows just how to care of you. With his knowledge and experience, Dr. J. Whitley Wills will work with you to help you achieve all of your dental goals. Dr. J. Whitley Wills also utilizes some of the latest technology available to improve the health of your smile. Whether your are missing teeth and require dental implants or you are visiting for a routine exam, you know that you are in good hands at our office.
